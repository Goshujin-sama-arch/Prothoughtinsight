Top Strategies to Grow Your YouTube Channel

By Jaja Victor Barisuka | Published on January 16, 2025

Hey there, future YouTube star! So, you've started a YouTube channel, uploaded a few videos, and... crickets. Yeah, growing on YouTube can feel like yelling into the void, but don’t worry. I’ve got you covered. Let’s talk about some simple (but powerful) strategies to grow your YouTube channel and start racking up those views and subscribers!

1. Find Your Niche and Stick to It

YouTube is a big place. If you're making videos about gaming today and cooking tomorrow, people won’t know what to expect. Pick a niche you’re passionate about and stick to it. This way, viewers know exactly why they should subscribe to you.

2. Create Eye-Catching Thumbnails

Let’s be real—people judge a video by its thumbnail. Design bold, clear, and eye-catching thumbnails that make people WANT to click. Use bright colors, readable text, and close-up faces with strong emotions. Canva is your friend here!

3. Optimize Video Titles and Descriptions (SEO Magic)

Think like a viewer. What would YOU search for to find your video? Use those keywords in your title and description. For example, instead of “My Fitness Journey,” try “How I Lost 20 Pounds in 3 Months (No Gym)”. Keywords make your videos searchable!

4. Hook Viewers in the First 10 Seconds

YouTube rewards watch time, so if viewers leave after a few seconds, your video won't go far. Start with a hook! Ask a question, tease the value, or show something exciting right away. Keep them glued to the screen.

5. Be Consistent with Uploads

If you’re uploading once every two months, people will forget you exist. Set a schedule (weekly, bi-weekly—whatever works) and stick to it. Consistency builds trust and anticipation.

6. Use Playlists to Boost Watch Time

Playlists aren’t just for organizing videos; they’re great for increasing watch time. Create themed playlists to keep viewers watching your content back-to-back. More watch time = better ranking on YouTube.

7. Engage with Your Audience

Reply to comments, ask questions in your videos, and encourage viewers to share their thoughts. The more engagement your videos get, the more YouTube will push them to new viewers.

8. Collaborate with Other YouTubers

Don’t go solo! Collaborations are a great way to tap into another creator’s audience. Find YouTubers in your niche and propose fun collab ideas. It’s a win-win for both channels.

9. Add Call-to-Actions (CTAs)

People need reminders! Tell viewers to like, comment, and subscribe. But don’t just say it—make it fun. For example, “Smash that like button like it owes you money!” You get the idea.

10. Promote Your Videos on Social Media

Don’t rely on YouTube alone. Share your videos on Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, Twitter—anywhere your audience hangs out. Short clips or teasers work great for grabbing attention on other platforms.

11. Analyze and Improve with YouTube Analytics

YouTube gives you free data—USE IT! Check your analytics to see what’s working and what’s not. Look at watch time, click-through rate, and audience retention. Then, tweak your content accordingly.

Final Thoughts

Growing a YouTube channel isn’t instant, but it’s 100% doable if you stay consistent and smart. Focus on delivering value, optimizing your content, and engaging with your audience. Before you know it, that subscriber count will start climbing!
